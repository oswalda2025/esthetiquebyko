Our Story

Where
Craft Meets
Couture

Ko Esthetique was born from a love of fashion and fine art — and the belief that the two should never be kept apart. Every piece we create is hand-painted from scratch, making each one a singular work of art.

We use only the finest American paints — chosen for their vibrancy, permanence, and the way they hold a brushstroke with the confidence of a master's hand.

From silk sarees and elegant suits to shoes, purses, jackets, and t-shirts — if you can wear it, we can paint it.
